CeraVe Moisturizing Cream is a deeply hydrating, dermatologist-developed formula designed to restore and strengthen the skin’s natural barrier. Infused with three essential ceramides and hyaluronic acid, it locks in moisture, providing lasting hydration that leaves skin feeling soft and supple throughout the day.

What sets CeraVe Moisturizing Cream apart is its MVE (Multivesicular Emulsion) Technology, which allows for controlled release of hydrating ingredients over 24 hours, ensuring a steady stream of moisture. The cream is non-comedogenic, fragrance-free, and gentle enough for sensitive skin, making it suitable for use on both the face and body. Its rich, non-greasy texture absorbs quickly, creating a smooth finish that makes it easy to incorporate into any skincare routine without leaving a sticky residue.

Aqua/Water, Glycerin, Cetearyl Alcohol, Caprylic/Capric Triglyceride, Cetyl Alcohol, Ceteareth-20, Petrolatum, Dimethicone, Phenoxyethanol, Behentrimonium Methosulfate, Potassium Phosphate, Ethylhexylglycerin, Sodium Lauroyl Lactylate, Disodium EDTA, Dipotassium Phosphate, Ceramide NP, Ceramide AP, Phytoshingosine, Cholesterol, Xanthan Gum, Carbomer, Sodium Hyaluronate, Tocopherol, Ceramide EOP

  • Provides long-lasting hydration for dry and sensitive skin
  • Helps restore the skin's natural protective barrier
  • Non-greasy formula absorbs quickly into the skin
  • Fragrance-free, making it suitable for sensitive skin

Apply liberally to the face and body, as needed, or as recommended by a healthcare provider. Can be used morning and night for optimal hydration results.

  • For external use only.
  • Avoid contact with eyes.
  • If irritation occurs, discontinue use.
  • Store in a cool, dry place.
