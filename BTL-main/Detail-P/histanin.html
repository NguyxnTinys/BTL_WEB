Kerala Ayurveda Histantin Tablet (Pack of 10 x 10's) are a natural remedy derived from traditional Ayurvedic formulations to offer relief from allergies and respiratory concerns. These tablets are crafted to provide a holistic approach to managing allergic symptoms without the use of artificial chemicals, making them safe for long-term use.

Whether it's pollen allergies in the spring, dust-induced running nose and skin rashes, or year-round irritants, you'll enjoy a life free from allergies and experience a refreshing and revitalised feeling. Forget about the chronic sniffles and sneezing and start feeling better, naturally. Histantin Tablets are easy to take, so you can take them anywhere, anytime, and be prepared to tackle any allergy challenges that come your way.

  • Bamboo silica (Vamshalochanam): Helps ease colds and running nose with its anti-allergic properties.
  • Green chiretta (Bhunimba): Helps regulate immune response and cleanses the body.
  • Turmeric (Haridra): Helps to ease allergy symptoms by balancing the pitta dosha.
  • Sickle pod (Prapunnada): Highly beneficial for skin allergies.
  • Offers natural relief from allergy symptoms
