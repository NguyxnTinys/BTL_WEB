Precaution

The I PILL TABLET is an emergency contraceptive designed to prevent pregnancy after unprotected intercourse. It contains Levonorgestrel, a hormone that can delay ovulation, making it less likely for fertilization to occur.

For best results, it should be taken as soon as possible after unprotected sex, preferably within 72 hours. The tablet is safe and effective when used as directed.
