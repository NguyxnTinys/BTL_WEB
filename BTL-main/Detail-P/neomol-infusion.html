Neomol I.V Infusion contains Paracetamol which belongs to the group of medicines called Analgesics and Antipyretics. It is used for short-term management of moderate pain (especially after surgery) and fever when administered by the intravenous route is clinically justified.

Neomol I.V Infusion contains 'Paracetamol,' which prevents the formation of chemical messengers called prostaglandins, which cause pain and swelling at the injury sites. This process reduces the mild to moderate pain and inflammation at the injured or damaged site. Neomol Infusion also affects an area of the brain that regulates body temperature known as the hypothalamic heat-regulating centre. Thus, it reduces fever.

Neomol I.V Infusion should be used with caution in pregnant and breastfeeding women only if clearly advised by the doctor. NEOMOL I.V INFUSION should be used with caution in children and elderly patients.

Paracetamol (Acetaminophen) 1000mg/100ml, Sodium (as sodium hydroxide): QS to adjust pH, Sterile water for injection.
